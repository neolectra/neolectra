Fulgora XE Neoquad

Very much like the XE, but the Neoquad is equipped with an all-wheel drive and enhanced sensor proximity technology. XE Neoquad drivers strive to protect themselves and the environment. They are forward-thinking and cautious, living consciously and taking nothing for granted in life, including the planet itself.

Emotion

A sense of security is established when driving the XE with Neoquad equipped. Psychological stability while driving reassures the worried and improves the driving experiene significantly. The moral benefit of saving the environment is certainly a welcome one.

Design

Implementing all-wheel-drive on a light-framed car maximizes the precision of stopping and starting, as well as utilizing basic physics laws to improve acceleration in snowy climates. A familiar interior reminiscent of old Fulgora models is a tribute to Neolectra’s rich history.

Interior

The technological look of carbon fiber, featured in the monocoque as well as the doors, seats, center tunnel and console, coexists with a design of clear aeronautical inspiration, while the leather and upholstery adds a bold touch to the luxurious interior.

It is the refined details of the cross-stitching and special Neolectra plaque that confer a truly unique character to this remarkable engineering gem.

Driving

All wheel drive on the most environmentally friendly vehicle in the world is a match made in heaven. Built for the winter, its ability to withstand extreme climates can be attributed to the high horsepower/torque-to-weight ratio. The light frame makes high control possible
