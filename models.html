Fulgora

Energy Efficient – Fulgora, the Roman goddess of lightning, is a perfect representation of our flagship sedan. Like the lightning bolt that she symbolizes, each of the three Fulgora models’ distinguishing feature is their energy-efficiency. With the largest all-electric-range and quickest charge speeds out of any Neolectra vehicle, the Fulgora is the embodiment of the transition to clean, renewable energy sources. With a simple, minimalistic exterior, coupled with a sleek interior, this truly is the jack of all trades. The Fulgora contains three subcategories, each with different specialities. The base model, the Fulgora XE, puts the utmost emphasis on environmentalism, with improved economy and dynamics. The Fulgora XE Neoquad is equipped with four-wheel drive, perfect for snowy or rainy climates. The Fulgora XE Tergo is equipped with Tergo™, an ultra-responsive steering system that balances the toll of a turn across front and rear wheels, allowing for a more effective turn, as well as delivering extra power to the rear wheels.

Bronte

Built for Speed – Bronte is the name of the Greek goddess of thunder. Like the goddess, the Neolectra Bronte’s loud, aggressive engine is a trademark feature. Its speed and acceleration are remarkable, and it has one of the world’s fastest 0-60 mph accelerations at 2.1 seconds. Whether it’s on the highway or on the racetrack, the Bronte is a fierce vehicle with a boisterous personality. The Bronte contains three subcategories, each with different specialites. The Bronte XFE Nitro is Neolectra’s premier street-racetrack vehicle, with unbeatable horsepower and acceleration. The Bronte XF Neoquad is equipped with four-wheel drive, perfect for snowy or rainy climates. The Bronte XF Domus is a non-traditional racing vehicle – it takes the form of an SUV, still with incredible horsepower and speed for its size. It expands the thrill of a racecar into a large, shareable experience.

Astrape

Family First – Astrape is the Greek goddess of lightning, is as bold and daring as the Neolectra Astrape. The Astrape is Neolectra’s flagship SUV, and it emphasizes safety, spaciousness, and family fun. Backseat features are almost entirely the same as the features in the front seats, whereas many other companys neglect the backseat passengers. The Astrape has expansive cargo and leg room, making it the most comfortable vehicle that Neolectra has to offer. The Astrape is a standalone car that is perfect for any family activity, from road trips and long-haul rides to soccer practice with the kids.

Astrape

Pele

Sleekness, Revolutionized - Pele, the Hawaiian goddess of volcanoes, directly represents the Neolectra Pele; while it may lie dormant and quiet, it suddenly erupts unexpectedly, its blistering explosiveness shocking onlookers. The Neolectra Pele is a quiet, energy conserving vehicle that remains silent until the driver presses the accelerator. The supercharger within creates an aggressive noise as the quick acceleration causes an enormous increase in speed. The Pele is a standalone vehicle with no subcategories and is an impressive, compact two-seater that features a stunning design with a rich surround sound system that resonates throughout the compact interior.

Pele

Oya

Luxury Reinvented. The Neolectra Oya is of the highest quality in terms of luxuriousness. Everything about the car’s feel and design has been streamlined, with improved aerodynamics, to meet the highest standard of luxury. Its complexity is embedded deep within every aspect of the vehicle. The sleek interior and metallic exterior speak for themselves, working in tandem to create a cohesive masterpiece.
