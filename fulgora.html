Fulgora

Energy Efficient – Fulgora, the Roman goddess of lightning, is a perfect representation of our flagship sedan. Like the lightning bolt that she symbolizes, each of the three Fulgora models’ distinguishing feature is their energy-efficiency. With the largest all-electric-range and quickest charge speeds out of any Neolectra vehicle, the Fulgora is the embodiment of the transition to clean, renewable energy sources. With a simple, minimalistic exterior, coupled with a sleek interior, this truly is the jack of all trades. The Fulgora contains three subcategories, each with different specialities. The base model, the Fulgora XE, puts the utmost emphasis on environmentalism, with improved economy and dynamics. The Fulgora XE Neoquad is equipped with four-wheel drive, perfect for snowy or rainy climates. The Fulgora XE Tergo is equipped with Tergo™, an ultra-responsive steering system that balances the toll of a turn across front and rear wheels, allowing for a more effective turn, as well as delivering extra power to the rear wheels

Design

The Fulgora embraces a minimalistic, simple design. It inherits a clean, functional design from preceding Fulgora models of previous years. Each Fulgora features comfortable, cushioned seats and a spacious cargo. Everything within the Neolectra Fulgora has been optimized to save energy in every nook and cranny of the vehicle. The Fulgora is functional, solid, and an all-rounder electric car.
