Sammy Urtzal

Sammy Urtzal, the founder of the Neolectra brand, is a city girl and a great lover of fast cars, a down-to-earth entrepreneur, and a visonary woman all at the same time.

Peyton Everlue

Peyton Everlue, second-in-command, Sammy's best friend, and the ever essential Chief Operating Officer. She's a country girl and totally in love with everything cars.

Joselyn Sputak

Joselyn Sputak, a book lover, a money lover, and the best Chief Financial Officer around. In her spare time, she reads about mermaids and conspiracy theories.

Dubbed "The Child Genius" at age 2, Laura Chen continues to blow everyone's mind with her intelligence. An intellectual, absolute prodigy, and the world's best Secretary.
