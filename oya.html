Oya

Luxury Reinvented

The Neolectra Oya is of the highest quality in terms of luxuriousness. Everything about the car’s feel and design has been streamlined, with improved aerodynamics, to meet the highest standard of luxury. Its complexity is embedded deep within every aspect of the vehicle. The sleek interior and metallic exterior speak for themselves, working in tandem to create a cohesive masterpiece.

Emotion

Close your eyes. Imagine a professionally dressed businessperson. Perhaps a spy. They draw out their keys from their pocket, and brandish it as if it was a sword. They coolly slip into the driver’s seat under the inky blackness of the night sky. They drive across the empty highway, anonymously, silently. Instinctively, they press the accelerator and as if it were a rocket ship blasting through space, the car takes off. They feel the rush, yet they remain cool and collected. They reach their destination and park the car, not making a sound.

Design

The Oya truly is a once-in-a-lifetime car. A chamfered hood, streamlined body, and a trademark spoiler are the defining features of the Oya. It combines rich aerodynamics and a beautiful body with the practicality of a typical sedan. But, this is not your everyday car. Its surround sound system and elegant interior are loved by fans, with elegant mood lighting to top it off.

Interior

The Neolectra Oya is the purest example of connection, of interaction with the machine through physical contact and the senses. Its advanced interior features an 8.4-inch touchscreen system integrated in the center console, offering a sophisticated on-board experience. The upholstery is luxurious and made of carbon-fiber material available exclusively from Neolectra.

Driving

The Oya is big on smoothness. Everything from the buttery smooth brakes and accelerator and the controlled steering make the Oya a true pleasure to drive. Oya drivers have commented on their newfound sense of professionalism as they drive their vehicle. Drivers tend to be relaxed, mature, and composed.
