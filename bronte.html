Bronte

Built for Speed

Bronte is the name of the Greek goddess of thunder. Like the goddess, the Neolectra Bronte’s loud, aggressive engine is a trademark feature. Its speed and acceleration are remarkable, and it has one of the world’s fastest 0-60 mph accelerations at 2.1 seconds. Whether it’s on the highway or on the racetrack, the Bronte is a fierce vehicle with a boisterous personality. The Bronte contains three subcategories, each with different specialties. The Bronte XFE Nitro is Neolectra’s premier street-racetrack vehicle, with unbeatable horsepower and acceleration. Every Bronte XFE Nitro car is, by default, equipped with the NeoBoost engine, the premium and most powerful engine. The Bronte XF Neoquad is equipped with four-wheel drive, perfect for snowy or rainy climates. The Bronte XF Domus is a non-traditional racing vehicle – it takes the form of an SUV, still with incredible horsepower and speed for its size. It expands the thrill of a racecar into a large, shareable experience.

Design

The Bronte’s signature low, aggressive demeanor reflects its attitude on the roads. Its roaring engine draws attention and fascinated stares from passerby and onlookers. It has an indented hood, Neolectra’s signature look. It has been meticulously crafted to tear through its competitors, with incredible horsepower and speed. The Bronte is powerful, animal-like, and vicious.
