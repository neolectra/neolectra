Pele

Sleekness, Revolutionized - Pele, the Hawaiian goddess of volcanoes, directly represents the Neolectra Pele; while it may lie dormant and quiet, it suddenly erupts unexpectedly, its blistering explosiveness shocking onlookers. The Neolectra Pele is a quiet, energy conserving vehicle that remains silent until the driver presses the accelerator. The supercharger within creates an aggressive noise as the quick acceleration causes an enormous increase in speed. The Pele is a standalone vehicle with no subcategories and is an impressive, compact two-seater that features a stunning design with a rich surround sound system that resonates throughout the compact interior.

Emotion

You glace at your gold-plated watch to check the time, with your right hand on the steering wheel and the left hanging out the window as you watch the plains fly by on the cool summer evening. As you feel the breeze whip through your hair, you lower your sunglasses and gaze off into the mountainside. As the car accelerates, your body is as calmed and collected as your Neolectra Pele.

Design

Being a fan favorite, the Pele is a two-seater convertible that features a compact body, with limited storage space. It has a stunning glossy finish in a wide range of different colors. Its rounded body adds a classiness to the exterior, and leather interiors with a smooth, matte black finish. The Pele is quiet, yet explosive.

Interior

The passenger compartment features a cutting-edge 8.4-inch touchscreen system in the center console, which is where the vehicle and entertainment functions are controlled.

Driving

The Pele offers a unique combination of the active steering wheel system and the rear-steering system, ensuring top-of-the-line dynamic performance. After one drive, you'll be tempted to go on another one. With the Pele, there won't be any stopping. You'll be so addicted to the feeling of driving that you'll forget who you are, where you are, and what you're doing. It's just that amazing.
