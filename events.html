Neolectra Events

2021 Fulgora Z Release

Dec 10, 2020

Location: Neolectra Design and Research Studio - Los Angeles, CA


Join us on December 10, 2020 for the release of the newest model from our Fulgora line, the 2021 Fulgora Z! We will be livestreaming the event on pay-per-view as well, so make sure to catch us there if you can’t make it. Press seating will be reserved, so make sure you buy your tickets fast.

Sept 12, 2020

Neolectra International Code Day Workshop

Location: Varies (see below)


To celebrate international code day and as a part of our initiative to inspire the next generation of the STEM workforce, Neolectra is hosting a FREE two-hour coding workshop! It will be on September 12, 2020 from 11am-1pm in select cities (see below)


  • Los Angeles
  • San Francisco
  • New York
  • Miami
  • Chicago
  • Dallas
  • Seattle
  • Boston

The event is open to anyone ages 3 -17 who would like to learn the basics of code, meet other like-minded coders, or just come hang out with our Neolectra engineers. At the end of the day, the goal is to just have fun coding and talking to other people, so please feel free to join us!

Mar 8, 2020

Neolectra International Women's Day Convention

Location: Anaheim Convention Center

As a part of our Girl-Powered initiative, Neolectra invites you to join us in our International Women’s Day convention! We will be having many planned events, including talks from successful female entrepreneurs and engineers, as well as leadership workshops dedicated to combatting gender bias in the workplace and guides on how to make a name for yourself as a woman in the STEM workforce. This event will run all day, from 9am to 9pm on Sunday, March 8, 2020, in the Anaheim Convention Center. Some of the workshops we are running will be livestreamed on our YouTube channel, so make sure to go check it out if you can’t make it. We hope to see you soon in Anaheim!
