Bronte XF Domus

The Bronte XF Domus is perhaps one of the least traditional racing cars you will come across in your lifetime. Imagine the explosive speed of a racing car, and then combine it with the communal, familial experience of a sports utility vehicle. In the XF Domus, barriers in the transportation industry have been broken. A monstrous engine and meticulous engineering effort have been put into the creation of the XF Domus. This car is for all the wild, fun loving families that want both thrill and togetherness.

Emotion

The Domus makes families come alive. There’s nothing like the experience of sharing a thrill. It’s like a portable roller coaster! Families are ecstatic both on their road trips and their regular daily drives. The generous spaciousness is coupled with a lavish interior that truly doesn’t make many sacrifices. XF Domus drivers are fun-loving and they live in the moment.

Design

The XF Domus is like a passenger train – large, fast, and full of people. For an SUV, its engine in unmatched, attracting amazed eyes as it speeds along. With exceptional horsepower and a top speed of 180 mph, the Domus’ speed is no joke. Spacious cargo and back seats coupled with a variety of in-car features make it truly a standout car. It comes in three simple, bold colors.

Interior

The Bronte XF Domus features subtle and luxurious interiors in a variety of colors. Each detail has been refined to reach the best engineering solution for an optimal driving experience also in terms of cabin comfort. This optimal design and function is reached with the implmentation of a channel located in the rear wall, in the back of the headrest, for the air to flow smoothly from the cabin towards the rear of the bonnet and to minimize turbulence within the passenger compartment. The rear wing then benefits from additional aero-load for increased rear downforce.

Driving

Driving the XF Domus is something of a roller coaster ride in which the driver remains relaxed and in total control. Despite being a fast, large car, power steering and adaptive cruise control make it a calming, controlled experience for the driver. It’s equipped with an aggressive, loud engine that draws attention. Families that driver the XF Domus truly are the boldest folks around.
