Biography

Everlue grew up in the suburbs of Manhattan, New York. A keen lover of the outdoors, she spent most of her early life and childhood playing outside and exploring nature. One of her most vivid memories from her early teenage years was the day she found out that the Island Fox, her favorite animal, was officially classified as endangered. She was overwhelmed with feelings of fear and determination to turn the atrocity of global warming around. She began silently critiquing the way humans consumed energy.
